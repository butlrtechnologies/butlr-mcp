# Code of Conduct

We expect all participants to be respectful and professional in their interactions. Be kind, constructive, and considerate.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, and publishing others' private information without consent.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please email **support@butlr.io**. All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled confidentially.
